realmem(size=4096)

 
Returns:
Actual free memory. This works by allocating blocks of size size until malloc() returns NULL.

battery()

 

Returns a tuple (plugged, present, charging, lifep, lifet, temp, volt), where:

  • plugged is 1 if the PSP is plugged in, 0 else.
  • present is 1 if the battery is present, 0 else.
  • charging is 1 if the battery is charging, 0 else.
  • lifep is the life of the battery, in percentage.
  • lifet is the life of the battery, in minutes (0 if it's plugged).
  • temp is the battery temperature (celsius)
  • volt is the battery voltage.
